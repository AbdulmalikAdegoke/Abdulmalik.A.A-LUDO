Guide Lines

Player order

1. First player: Red, Second Player: Green, Third Player: Yellow, Fourth Player: Blue

How To Play

1. Roll Dice

2. If none of the dice are 6 then turn is passed if no piece has been brought out yet

2.1 However if a piece has already been brought out then a move can be made

3. If your piece lands on a spot already occupied by your opponent the opponent is captured and sent back to their base

4. The winner is the first person to get four pieces to their home

How to make a move

1. After dice has been rolled and current player can make a move, you click on the die that you want to make use of for the move

2. After a die has been used it cannot be revisited

3. You have to use both die if they are usable
